ABOUT US

Mission

To reach out as many people as possible and make a difference in their life. Our mission is to make their inner self happy. We want to bring a change in society by trying to eliminate the discrimination of victims of FGM, GBV and Mental illness.

Values

• Transparency
• Respect for human rights
• No Discriminatory
• Commitment
• Integrity
• No personal benefit
• Secular, Non-Political and Non-Religious

Vision

To make this world a better place for unprivileged people by providing them whatever required to our full potential. We believe that talent and skills are God’s gift and are product of hard work and not birth. To fill the society with individuals who has the power of differentiating between what is good and bad for them as well as the society.

Our approach

Our Story

We reach out to people dealing with anxiety and depression to show them that life is worth living and also showing them that we are aware that it's a mental illness and we're here to help them. We also want to reach out to rural places where people are still Naive and still practicing GBV and FGM to educate them on the consequences.
